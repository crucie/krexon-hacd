# KREXON — Issuer FAQ

---

## Formation basics

**What is a Stack lot?**
A Stack lot is one issuance unit in KREXON's formation structure. Each lot requires 1 HACD unit as the container and 100 HAC as the formation cost. Completing a Stack transaction forms 100,000 KRX attributed to that HACD lot.

**Why does KREXON use HACD and not just deploy on Solana directly?**
A Solana SPL token can be deployed in minutes with no cost and no history. Loyalty program partners — hotels, airlines, retailers — will ask why they should trust a token representing their brand. HACD Stack formation answers that: every KRX unit traces to a specific HACD with a visible, on-chain formation cost. That is the credibility argument a B2B partner will accept.

**How does the supply get to Solana?**
KRX is formed on the HACD layer through Stack issuance. A bridge or wrap mechanism moves KRX to Solana as an SPL token for AMM participation. The specific bridge mechanism is being confirmed — check the Launchpad for current status.

**Are all lots the same?**
Yes, in the default structure. All 1,000 HACD lots form 100,000 KRX each at a stack cost of 100 HAC. A tiered partner lot structure is possible — this requires issuer confirmation before Launchpad.

## Participant requirements

**Do I need HACD?**
Yes. Each Stack participation requires at least 1 HACD unit. HACD is what gives KRX its PoW-backed formation credential.

**Do I need HAC?**
Yes. HAC is the Hacash network currency used to pay the stack cost (100 HAC per HACD) and network fees. Network fees vary — check live at explorer.hacash.org.

**Where do I get HACD and HAC?**
Check the official Hacash resources at hacash.org. Do not purchase from unverified sources. KREXON and HACD Labs will never ask for your seed phrase, private key, or remote access to your device.

## Token utility

**What does KRX do?**
KRX serves three roles in the KREXON ecosystem: governance (voting on platform decisions), liquidity incentives (earning rewards by providing AMM liquidity), and fee capture (a share of DEX swap fees flows to KRX holders). Partner access tiers may also require KRX.

**Is utility live at launch?**
Utility availability at launch is to be confirmed by the issuer. Not all utilities may be active at the time of initial formation.

## Safety and compliance

**Is this an investment?**
No. KREXON is a HACD Stack Asset launch. KRX is a utility and governance token. No return on investment is guaranteed. Stack cost is a formation input — not a guaranteed redemption value.

**Will KREXON ever ask for my seed phrase or private key?**
Never. KREXON, HACD Labs, and the HACD Incubator will never ask for seed phrases, private keys, wallet passwords, or remote access to your device. Any message asking for these is a scam.

**What are the regulatory risks?**
Loyalty point tokenization may be subject to financial regulation in some jurisdictions. Participants should consult qualified legal counsel before participating. KREXON is not providing legal or financial advice.
