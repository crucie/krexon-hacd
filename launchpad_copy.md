## What is KREXON?

KREXON (KRX) is the base token of the KREXON loyalty exchange — a decentralized platform where loyalty points from hotels, airlines, and retail programs can be swapped, pooled, and exchanged at real market rates.

The global loyalty points market holds over $700B in outstanding value. Most of it is trapped — locked inside siloed programs with poor redemption rates, expiry dates, and no secondary market.

KREXON changes that. By building on HACD Stack issuance and Solana's AMM infrastructure, KREXON gives points holders a real exit — and gives loyalty programs a credible, auditable token for their digital economy.

## How Stack Works

KREXON tokens are formed through HACD Stack issuance before they are available on Solana.

Each Stack lot works like this:

| Step | What happens |
|---|---|
| 1 | A participant holds 1 HACD unit (the PoW container) |
| 2 | The participant pays 100 HAC as the stack cost (formation input) |
| 3 | The Stack transaction is confirmed on-chain |
| 4 | 100,000 KRX are formed and attributed to that HACD lot |
| 5 | The HACD formation record is permanently inscribed |
| 6 | KRX is bridged to Solana for DEX participation |

**1,000 HACD lots. 100,000 KRX per lot. 100,000,000 KRX total supply.**

All lots follow the same rules. No hidden allocations. No preferential pricing.

## FAQ

**Do I need HACD to participate?**
Yes. Each Stack participation requires at least 1 HACD unit. HACD is the PoW container that makes KRX formation credible.

**Do I need HAC?**
Yes. HAC is the Hacash native currency used to pay the stack cost (100 HAC per HACD) and network fees.

**What is the difference between HACD and HAC?**
HACD is the PoW-mined asset container used as the Stack lot. HAC is the currency used to pay for the stack cost and network fees. Both are part of the Hacash ecosystem.

**Is KRX available immediately after formation?**
KRX is formed on the HACD layer. A bridge or wrap mechanism is used to make KRX available on Solana. The bridge mechanism will be confirmed before Launchpad launch.

**Can I participate if I am not a loyalty program?**
Yes. Any holder of HACD and HAC can participate in KREXON Stack formation as an individual.

**What happens if I remove my Stack?**
Unstacking returns your HACD unit. KRX already in circulation is unaffected. The formation record on-chain remains permanent.

**Is this an investment product?**
No. KREXON is a HACD Stack Asset launch. KRX is a utility and governance token. No profit is guaranteed. Stack cost is a formation input, not a guaranteed return.
